# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** October 16, 2025

## Introduction

DIForM ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

**Important:** This is a template privacy policy. You must customize it for your specific use case and consult with a legal professional before using it in production.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information

We may collect personally identifiable information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

- Register for an account
- Use our services
- Contact us for support
- Subscribe to our newsletter

This may include:
- Name
- Email address
- Microsoft account information (for Electron app)
- Company name
- Job title

### 2. Automatically Collected Information

When you use DIForM, we automatically collect certain information about your device and usage:

- **Log Data:** IP address, browser type, operating system, access times, pages viewed
- **Device Information:** Device type, unique device identifiers, operating system version
- **Usage Data:** Features used, tasks created, interactions with the application
- **Cookies and Tracking:** See our Cookie Policy below

### 3. Microsoft Graph Data

If you use the Electron app with Microsoft Graph integration:

- We access your Microsoft 365 data (emails, calendar, files) only with your explicit consent
- Access tokens are stored locally on your device
- We do not send your Microsoft data to our servers unless explicitly required for task processing
- You can revoke access at any time through your Microsoft account settings

### 4. AI Processing Data

When you use AI features:

- Task commands and context are processed by our AI service (Ollama, running locally by default)
- We may log anonymized task metadata for service improvement
- We do not use your data to train third-party AI models

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

1. **Service Delivery**
   - Providing and maintaining the application
   - Processing your tasks and commands
   - Authenticating your identity

2. **Service Improvement**
   - Understanding usage patterns
   - Developing new features
   - Fixing bugs and improving performance

3. **Communication**
   - Sending service updates
   - Responding to support requests
   - Sending marketing communications (with your consent)

4. **Security**
   - Detecting and preventing fraud
   - Monitoring for security threats
   - Enforcing our terms of service

5. **Legal Compliance**
   - Complying with legal obligations
   - Responding to legal requests

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage

- **Database:** User accounts and task history are stored in MongoDB
- **Cache:** Temporary data may be cached in Redis
- **Local Storage:** Some data is stored locally on your device (tokens, preferences)
- **Logs:** Application logs are stored on our servers for up to 90 days

### Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- ✅ Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- ✅ Encryption at rest (database encryption)
- ✅ Password hashing (bcrypt)
- ✅ JWT token authentication
- ✅ Rate limiting and DDoS protection
- ✅ Regular security audits
- ✅ Access controls and monitoring

**Note:** No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

### Rights for EU Users (GDPR)

You have the right to:

- ✅ **Access** your personal data
- ✅ **Rectify** inaccurate data
- ✅ **Erase** your data ("right to be forgotten")
- ✅ **Restrict** processing
- ✅ **Data portability** (export your data)
- ✅ **Object** to processing
- ✅ **Withdraw consent** at any time

### Rights for California Users (CCPA)

You have the right to:

- ✅ Know what personal information we collect
- ✅ Request deletion of your data
- ✅ Opt-out of data sale (we don't sell data)
- ✅ Non-discrimination for exercising your rights

### How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: **privacy@diform.example.com**

We will respond within 30 days.
